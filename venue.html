Public Transportation & Access

Town Center:

The school is located within walking distance from Lüleburgaz town center (approx. 10-15 mins).

Local Minibus (Dolmuş):

You can use the 1st or 2nd line city minibuses that pass through Eski İstasyon Avenue.

Intercity:

From Lüleburgaz Bus Terminal (Otogar), you can reach the venue by a short taxi ride or local minibuses heading towards the center.
