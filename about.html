Our Story

Started as a visionary school project that only sends their students to local MUNs and teaches them how to MUN—to an organization that took us almost a year to complete to reach a dream we've barely dared to imagine before.

Today, RAYMUN stands as a testament to student leadership, unwavering dedication, and the power of international cooperation. We are here to create a platform where every voice finds its resonance.
