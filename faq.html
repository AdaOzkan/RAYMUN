F A Q

Answers before you pack

Still unsure about something? Browse the common questions below or reach our team via the contact page.

What are the official rules of procedure (RoP) of RAYMUN?
RAYMUN will follow the HarvardMUN rules of procedure to ensure a professional and globally recognized debate environment.
Is RAYMUN suitable for the non-experienced?
Yes! We welcome delegates of all levels. We have specific committees designed for first-timers (GAs) where chairs will provide extra guidance on procedure.
Is it mandatory to participate in all the sessions?
To receive a participation certificate, you must attend at least 5 out of 6 sessions. Missing 3 or more sessions will disqualify you from receiving awards or certificates.
What is the dress code?
The dress code is Western Business Attire (WBA). This includes suits, blazers, dress shirts, ties, and formal dresses or skirts.
Where will the conference be held?
At Ramazan Yaman Science High School, Lüleburgaz. Detailed directions and a map can be found on our Venue page.
Will there be accommodation provided?
No, RAYMUN does not provide official accommodation. However, we can recommend nearby hotels if you contact our PR team.
