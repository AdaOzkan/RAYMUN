Faq

Answers before you pack

"Where vision becomes voice"

What are the official rules of procedure (RoP) of RAYMUN?
RAYMUN will follow the HarvardMUN rules of procedure.
Is RAYMUN suitable for the non-experienced?
Yes, our conference has committees suitable for first-timers. It's up to your choice but many first-timers start with GA’s.
Is it mandatory to participate in all the sessions?
No, you can be absent for 2 sessions. If you were absent for 3 sessions, you won't be receiving an award; if you were absent for 4 sessions, you won't receive a participation certificate.
What is the dress code?
Our dress code is Western Business Attire.
Where will the conference be held?
At Ramazan Yaman Science High School, Lüleburgaz. You can find more information about the address on the venue page.
Will there be accommodation provided?
No, unfortunately there won’t be any accommodation provided.
Are international students allowed to apply?
No, unfortunately. We are currently unable to accept applications from outside Türkiye.
How can we learn more about the conference?
You can contact us via e-mail for any further questions through the e-mail address provided in the contact us section.
